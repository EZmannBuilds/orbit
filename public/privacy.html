Privacy Policy

Effective 21 July 2026 · Last updated 21 July 2026

Orbit Axis calculates astrology charts from your birth details. Those details are personal, and this page says exactly what is stored, why, who else can see it, and how to remove all of it.

On this page

    Who publishes Orbit Axis

    Orbit Axis is published by not yet published. For privacy questions, contact not yet published.

    What you provide

    • Email address and password — to create and access your account. Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth and are never stored by Orbit in readable form.
    • Birth date, birth time, and birthplace — the inputs a chart is calculated from.
    • Coordinates and time zone — derived from the birthplace you select, so historical daylight-saving rules can be applied correctly.
    • Chart names and saved charts — including charts you create for other people.
    • Preferences — such as your detail level.
    • Questions you ask Ask Orbit, and the conversations they belong to.
    • Anything you send to support.

    If you create a chart for somebody else, you are providing their birth details. Please only do that for people who would be comfortable with it.

    What Orbit Axis calculates from it

    • Natal chart placements, houses, angles, and aspects
    • Transits, synastry, and the current sky
    • Daily readings, the evidence behind them, and your reading history
    • Ask Orbit answers and the evidence they were built from
    • Which chart is active, and when you last selected it

    These are stored so your charts and readings are the same on every device you sign in from.

    Technical information

    Orbit keeps deliberately little:

    • Session information — an authentication cookie that keeps you signed in. It cannot be read by scripts in your browser.
    • Request identifiers — a random per-request id used to trace an error you report. It is not linked to you.
    • Error information — the error code and route. Request bodies are never logged, so your birth details do not appear in Orbit's logs.
    • Standard server request data handled by the hosting provider.

    There is no analytics, no advertising, no tracking pixel, and no third-party profiling. Orbit does not sell or share your data, because there is nobody to share it with.

    Services Orbit relies on

    ServiceWhat it handles
    SupabaseAccounts, authentication, and the database holding your charts and readings. Also sends account emails such as password resets.
    VercelHosting and serving the application.
    GeoapifyBirthplace lookup. Receives the place text you type, so it can return coordinates. It does not receive your identity or birth date.
    Swiss Ephemeris (Astrodienst)Astronomical calculation software. It runs inside Orbit and sends nothing anywhere.

    Ask Orbit's optional local language model runs only on a developer's own machine during development. It is not part of the public service, and no reading you receive is sent to an external AI provider. If that ever changes, this page will change with it before the feature ships.

    Who can see your data

    Only you. Every table holding user data enforces ownership in the database itself, so one account cannot read another's charts, readings, or conversations — that is checked automatically with two separate test accounts, not merely intended.

    How long it is kept

    • Your data is kept while your account exists.
    • Deleting your account removes it permanently. There is no soft delete, no grace period, and no recovery window.
    • Reference data — planetary event tables, tarot card definitions — is not yours and is unaffected.

    Orbit's hosting and database providers may retain their own operational backups for a short period under their standard practice. Orbit does not control those and cannot delete from them on request, so this page does not claim otherwise.

    Deleting your account

    In the app: More → Account → Delete account, then type DELETE to confirm. It removes your account, saved charts and birth details, readings and history, Ask Orbit conversations, and preferences, and clears the copy your browser had cached. See how account deletion works.

    Your controls

    • See and edit your saved charts at any time
    • Delete individual charts
    • Change your detail level and preferences
    • Reset your password from the sign-in screen
    • Sign out on any device
    • Delete your entire account permanently
    • Ask a question, or request a copy of your data, at not yet published

    Depending on where you live, you may have further rights under local data protection law. Contact support and Orbit will honour them.

    Age

    Orbit Axis is intended for people aged not yet published and over. It is not directed at children, and accounts are not knowingly created for them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact support and it will be removed.

    Changes to this policy

    If what Orbit collects or how it is used changes, this page is updated and the "last updated" date above changes with it. Material changes will be announced in the app before they take effect.
